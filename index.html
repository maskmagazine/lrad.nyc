LRAD is a collaboration between Mask Magazine and DesiringMachine Soundsystem. LRAD is a dance party. But more than that, it is an experiment in collective madness. A space for us who feel that all imaginable ways of existing are deemed virtually impossible by the idiosyncrasies of the world unfolding around us. The LRAD cannon is a “less lethal weapon” used by army and police against protesters around the world. Recently, it made it’s NYC debut during the Eric Garner protests. The LRAD sends focused, high-decibel sounds and sirens at deafening levels in an attempt to confuse and intimidate.

LRAD is an attempt to envision a sonic weapon of our own.
